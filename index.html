About Komsiri

A village shaped by harmony, education, and agriculture.

Komsiri is nestled in Jada, where Fulani and Chamba families live side by side with respect and shared purpose. Education is valued, farms are tended with care, and cattle remain at the heart of everyday life.

  • Peaceful multicultural community
  • Active farmers and cattle rearers
  • Strong focus on education and growth
